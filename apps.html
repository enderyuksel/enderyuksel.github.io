A little note on the history of my personal website!

I have been maintaining my website since 1998, and 2023 marks 25 years of my personal website. Below is a chronological hosting history:

  • [1998-1999] 8m.com my first personal website, I built as a high school student
  • [1999-2003] itu.edu.tr as an undergraduate student at ITU
  • [2003-2007] cs.itu.edu.tr as a research assistant at ITU
  • [2007-2011] dtu.dk as a PhD student at DTU
  • [2011-2013] pbworks.com as a postdoc at DTU
  • [2013 - 2023] sites.google.com during my time working for the financial industry
  • [2023 - present] github.io My website's latest home
