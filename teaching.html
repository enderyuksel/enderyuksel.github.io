Teaching

I am a Part-time Lecturer at the Department of Digitalisation, Copenhagen Business School (CBS). I teach Applied Programming on the MSc in Business Administration and Digital Business programme, where I cover a breadth of topics combining academic depth with industry relevance.

#Programming #SoftwareEngineering #DigitalBusiness #ComputerScience

Applied Programming
MSc in Business Administration and Digital Business

Copenhagen Business School, Department of Digitalisation

In this course, I bring together academic foundations and hands-on industry experience to prepare students for real-world software development. The curriculum covers:

  • Agile Software Development
  • Backend and Web API (using ASP.NET Core)
  • Relational Databases (using PostgreSQL)
  • Frontend (using Angular/TypeScript)
  • Information Security
  • Product Mindset
  • Scrum
  • Software Architecture
  • Software Design
  • Software Testing
  • User Experience
